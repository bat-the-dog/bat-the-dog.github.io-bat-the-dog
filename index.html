Who are Bat the Dog?

Not your average duo — nor would they wish to be…

Based in Midlands UK, Bat the Dog are Shalane and Rock n Roll Pete; professional Musicians that have dedicated their life’s works to music and performance. Honing skills over years, perfecting technical abilities, creating a unique sound and polished delivery. Bat the Dog have mastered live shows, captivating audiences across the Midlands and beyond. Get in touch now to hear how they can entertain your customers, and learn more about their musical expertise, repertoire, and performance rates.
