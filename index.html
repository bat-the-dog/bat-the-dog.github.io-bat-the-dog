So who are Bat the Dog?

Not your average duo - nor do they wish to be…

Based in midlands UK, Bat the Dog are Shalane and Rock n Roll Pete; professional Musicians that have dedicated their life’s works to music and performance. Honing skills over years, perfecting technical abilities, creating a unique sound and polished delivery. Bat the Dog have mastered live shows, captivating audiences across the midlands and beyond.
